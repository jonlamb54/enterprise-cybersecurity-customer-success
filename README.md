# Enterprise Cybersecurity Customer Success

This is how I run enterprise customer success.

I’ve owned $6M+ in ARR across cybersecurity customers, driving retention, expansion, and executive alignment. This repo breaks down the actual frameworks I use to create value, not just talk about it.

This is not theory. This is what works in real accounts.

## What you’ll find here

- How I run Executive Business Reviews that lead to decisions
- How I prove ROI using real customer data over time
- How I turn risk, incidents, and gaps into expansion opportunities
- How I drive adoption and reduce time to value
- How I partner with Product and Engineering without the usual friction

Customer success is not support.  
It’s revenue protection and growth.

If you can’t tie what you’re doing to retention, risk reduction, or expansion, it’s noise.
